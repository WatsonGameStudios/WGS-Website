About Watson Game Studios

Watson Game Studios is an Indie Game Studio run by Alex A. Watson.

I like to make silly little games and share them with people online and this is my way of doing it semi proffesionally. I dont use generative AI for the production of my games as, not because I'm particularly against generative AI but because I genuinly love every part of game development and dont see any point other than speed & efficiency to use it. I understand that this could possibly be a bit frustrating to people who want to play Hampstead right now but I ask of you to please be patient, the game will come out soon in the mean time I will try my best to make the game as good as possible ❤️.
